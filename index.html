Profesional Profile

Ryan Latture is a Ph.D. student in the Materials department at UC, Santa Barbara. He worked with the Correlated Electrons Materials group at Oak Ridge National Lab while obtaining his BS in Materials Science and Engineering at the University of Tennessee. Today, he develops cutting-edge finite element software and global optimization algorithms used in discovering optimal materials selection and design for lattice structures with applications including energy absorbtion and load bearing applications.
